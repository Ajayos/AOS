# 📜 AOS Trademark & Branding Policy

This document outlines how the AOS (Ajay O S Platform) name, logo, and brand assets may be used.

---

## 🏷️ Trademark Notice

"AOS" and "Ajay O S" are identifiers associated with the Ajayos platform ecosystem.

Unless otherwise specified, these names and related branding elements are protected and may not be used in ways that imply endorsement or affiliation without permission.

---

## ✅ Permitted Uses

You MAY:

- Reference AOS in blog posts or tutorials
- Create integrations that work with AOS
- Mention AOS in documentation
- Link to official AOS websites
- Build compatible tools (clearly labeled as third-party)

---

## ❌ Prohibited Uses

You MAY NOT:

- Use the AOS logo as your own branding
- Impersonate the AOS platform
- Create domains that confuse users
- Claim official affiliation without permission
- Modify branding assets to misrepresent identity

---

## 🧩 Third-Party Integrations

If you build tools or plugins:

- Clearly state they are unofficial
- Do not imply endorsement
- Use phrasing like:

  "Compatible with AOS"
  "Built for AOS ecosystem"

---

## 🎨 Brand Assets

Official logos, visual assets, and branding materials may be provided separately if available.

Do not recreate or alter official brand assets without approval.

---

## 🌐 Domain & Naming Protection

Domains or projects that closely resemble official Ajayos properties in a misleading manner may be challenged.

---

## 📩 Permissions

For branding or commercial use requests, contact:

branding@ajayos.com

---

## ⚖️ Enforcement

Misuse of AOS branding may result in:

- Removal requests
- Public clarification
- Legal escalation if necessary

---

AOS values collaboration and ecosystem growth, but brand integrity must be preserved.
