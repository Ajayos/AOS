# 🔑 Acceptable Use Policy (AUP)

This Acceptable Use Policy governs use of AOS services, applications, and APIs.

By using AOS services, you agree to comply with this policy.

---

## ✅ Permitted Use

You may use AOS services for lawful purposes, including:

- Application development
- Automation workflows
- Integrations
- Research and experimentation
- Educational purposes

---

## ❌ Prohibited Activities

You may NOT:

- Engage in illegal activity
- Distribute malware
- Attempt system exploitation
- Perform denial-of-service attacks
- Abuse automation systems
- Send unsolicited bulk communications
- Upload harmful or illegal content
- Impersonate others
- Circumvent security controls

---

## 📡 Network & Infrastructure Abuse

Activities that disrupt platform stability are strictly prohibited, including:

- Traffic flooding
- Resource exhaustion attempts
- Exploiting rate limits
- Unauthorized scanning

---

## 📂 Content Restrictions

You may not use AOS services to host or distribute:

- Illegal content
- Hateful or violent material
- Content violating intellectual property rights
- Harmful scripts or exploits

---

## 🔒 Enforcement

AOS reserves the right to:

- Suspend or terminate accounts
- Restrict API access
- Remove hosted content
- Report illegal activities to authorities
