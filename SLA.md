# Service Level Agreement (SLA)

This SLA defines service expectations for AOS platform services.

---

## 1. Service Availability

AOS targets:

- 99.5% monthly uptime for core services

Excludes:

- Scheduled maintenance
- Force majeure events
- Customer-side connectivity issues

---

## 2. Support Response Targets

| Severity | Description            | Response Time          |
| -------- | ---------------------- | ---------------------- |
| Critical | Service unavailable    | Within 24 hours        |
| High     | Major feature impacted | Within 48 hours        |
| Medium   | Partial degradation    | Within 3 business days |
| Low      | Minor issue            | Best effort            |

---

## 3. Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance may occur periodically.

Where possible:

- Advance notice will be provided
- Maintenance windows will be minimized

---

## 4. Incident Handling

For confirmed platform-level incidents:

- Root cause analysis may be conducted
- Mitigation steps implemented
- Preventative improvements applied

---

## 5. Exclusions

SLA does not apply to:

- Beta or experimental features
- Misuse of platform services
- Violations of AUP

---

## 6. Service Credits (Optional Section)

Service credits may be offered at AOS discretion.
